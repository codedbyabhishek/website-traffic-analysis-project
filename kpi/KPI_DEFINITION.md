# KPI Definitions (High-End)

- `conversion_rate`: fraction of sessions reaching purchase stage
- `engagement_rate`: sessions with >=120 sec and >=3 pageviews
- `bounce_rate`: sessions with <=1 pageview and <30 sec duration
- `revenue_per_session`: total revenue / total sessions
- `hourly_anomaly_scan`: z-score based detection on hourly sessions
- `funnel_metrics`: distribution of traffic by funnel stage for conversion analysis
